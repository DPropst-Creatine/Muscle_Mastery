Frequently Asked Questions

How can I access the full course?

The course is protected by a passcode. If you have purchased access, you should have received the passcode via email. If not, please contact us directly.

Is this course suitable for beginners?

Yes! The course is specifically designed for adults over 40 who are new to structured resistance training and supplementation. All recommendations are evidence-based and beginner-friendly.

Can I get personalized advice?

While this course provides general evidence-based recommendations, for personalized medical advice, please consult with your healthcare provider or contact us about individual consultation services.

How often is the course content updated?

The course content is regularly reviewed and updated to reflect the latest research in muscle health, nutrition, and supplementation for adults over 40.
