Course Overview

A comprehensive 5-lesson mini-course designed specifically for adults over 40 who want to combat age-related muscle loss and maintain strength and independence.

Lesson 1: Creatine Science and Safety Fundamentals

Master the evidence-based foundation of creatine supplementation including safety profiles, optimal dosing, and quality selection for adults over 40.

Lesson 2: Protein Requirements and Timing Strategies

Implement evidence-based protein intake protocols that support muscle protein synthesis and combat age-related anabolic resistance.

Design and implement power-focused resistance training programs that maximize functional capacity and reduce fall risk.

Lesson 4: Supplement Protocols and Meal Planning

Create personalized supplementation schedules and meal planning strategies that optimize muscle protein synthesis and energy metabolism.

Lesson 5: Long Term Success and Troubleshooting

Develop sustainable habits and problem-solving strategies for maintaining muscle health gains while addressing common obstacles.
